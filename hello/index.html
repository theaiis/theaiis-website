Our Story

Built From Curiosity

I started coding at 19 with zero formal training when my dad brought home a 6-channel PLC demo unit. I programmed it to run a drag strip Christmas tree light system and a lane winner indicator for my slot car track. That same curiosity — across BASIC, FORTRAN, COBOL, LUA, and C# over three decades — is what built this business.

I spent years in oil and gas, working away from home. My wife convinced me to pursue software full time so I'd be around more. Best decision we ever made.

The AI Integration Specialists was conceived while cleaning the cat litter boxes. The best ideas come from the most unglamorous moments.

We're based in Richmond, Texas — Fort Bend County — and we serve businesses right here in our backyard. When you call us, you talk to the person who built your system.
