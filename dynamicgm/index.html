Features

Built Different

Every competitor is cloud-based and subscription-gated. Dynamic GM is the only option for local, offline, group play.

Local Network — No Internet

Works completely offline on your home or game room network. No accounts, no cloud dependency, no outages mid-session.

One-Time Purchase

$5 per player, $40 for the server. That's it. No monthly subscription, no token packs, no paywalled features.

Works With Multiple Systems

Built around open licensing — not just D&D. Pathfinder, Call of Cthulhu, Shadowrun and more. Rulesets are loadable modules.

GM Override Panel

The organizer sees critical events — nat 1s, character death, major plot moments — and can accept, modify, or override the AI's decision.

Voice + Text Input

Players speak or type their actions. The AI hears the table the same way a human GM would. Public voice, private text — both supported.

Private Messaging

Players can send secret messages to the AI — just like passing a note to your GM. Whisper conversations, secret actions, hidden agendas.
